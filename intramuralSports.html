Intramural Sports

What are Intramural Sports?

  • Different sports that you can compete in on a team that you make using signup sheet.
  • You choose what you sign up for.
  • You learn to work together as a team.

You May Be Interested If...

  • You enjoy playing sports
  • You want to be on a sports team without the pressure of being on the school team
  • You want to play a sport with your friends
  • You want to get better at sports

Important Information

  • You need to get a sign up sheet to start a team, contact a coach for more information, and the sports are seasonal.
