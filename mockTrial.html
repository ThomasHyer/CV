Mock Trial

What Is Mock Trail?

  • Mock trail gives you a chance to participate in a realistic court like setting and compete with other schools in cases as attorneys and witnesses. Competitions may take place in a courtroom or other location with the another team, or virtually with your team in a room at the student’s school and the other school’s team connected through zoom.
  • Mock trial gives you a chance to improve their teamwork, prepare for future court experience, and have a fun time playing witnesses and attorneys.
  • Judging is based on individual performance not on the outcome of the trail.
  • Students will take the bus to trails at court rooms or other locations.
  • There is a practice trail before the competing with other teams to prepare everyone.
  • Mock Trial has an attorney coach to help everyone be prepared for the trail.

You May Be Interested In Mock Trial If ...

  • You enjoy acting.
  • You are interested in becoming an attorney
  • You enjoy mysteries
  • You enjoy being around other students
  • You want to get used to working with other people
  • You want to have a fun experience and make new friends
